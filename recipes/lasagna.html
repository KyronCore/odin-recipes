Lasagna

Home

Description

Classic Italian baked pasta layered with meat sauce, cheese, and pasta sheets.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook the meat sauce.
  2. Layer pasta, sauce, and cheese.
  3. Bake until golden.
  4. Let it rest before serving.
